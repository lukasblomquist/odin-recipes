Unbelievable Chicken

Description

This rich and moist chocolate cake is perfect for any occasion. It’s simple to make and packed with flavor.

Serve it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or fresh berries for the ultimate dessert experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour a 9x9 inch baking pan.
  3. In a bowl, mix together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking soda.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let cool before serving.
