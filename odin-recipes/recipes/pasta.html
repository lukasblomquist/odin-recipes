Unbelievable Pasta

Description

This homemade pasta recipe is consistently great and easy to make with flour, eggs, salt, olive oil, and water. Roll out by hand or use a pasta machine to cut dough into desired pasta shape.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • water
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Make the dough.
  3. Turn the dough out and knead it for 10 minutes.
  4. Let the dough rest.
  5. Divide the dough into balls.
  6. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough.
